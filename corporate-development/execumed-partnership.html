The ExecuMed Partnership

Doctor-led preventative healthcare, integrated with your team development

We've partnered with ExecuMed Healthcare, a doctor-led preventative healthcare and wellness provider based in Cape Town, to bring structured medical wellbeing programmes into our corporate development work. ExecuMed's approach differs from traditional one-off wellness days — they focus on ongoing, medically-led programmes designed to improve employee wellbeing, reduce absenteeism, and support long-term workforce performance. This page explains what's available to your team through the partnership.

The Partnership

What's available through the partnership

The following services are available to Team Connect clients through the ExecuMed partnership. Every engagement can be shaped to your organisation's specific priorities.

Employee Wellbeing Programmes

Structured, medically-led programmes delivered over time rather than one-off events. Designed to improve day-to-day wellbeing, reduce absenteeism, and build a healthier working culture. Typically combines regular screening, health tracking, and follow-up support.

Mental Health and Burnout Prevention

Clinical screening for burnout risk, mental health support pathways, and structured programmes designed to address workplace psychological health. Delivered by ExecuMed's clinical team.

Executive Wellness

Comprehensive medical assessments for senior leaders — cardiovascular, metabolic, and mental health markers, plus lifestyle and stress diagnostics. Designed for executives whose sustained performance matters to the organisation.

Health Screening

Team-wide preventative health checks including vitals, glucose, cholesterol, and other core biomarkers. Can be delivered on-site as a wellness day, or as part of an ongoing programme.

Additional capabilities

Also available through the partnership: telemedicine consultations for on-demand access to doctors, occupational medicals for roles that legally require them, and educational wellness sessions for teams.

Our Approach

How ExecuMed works with Team Connect

Integrated delivery, single point of contact

These services are delivered by ExecuMed's clinical team, integrated into your existing Team Connect engagement. That means you have a single point of contact for both your development work and your clinical wellbeing — one conversation, one plan, one coordinated delivery. Team Connect owns the client relationship; ExecuMed brings the clinical expertise.

Our Clinical Partner

About our clinical partner

ExecuMed Healthcare is a Cape Town-based, doctor-led preventative healthcare and wellness provider, focused on structured programmes that deliver measurable outcomes. Led by CEO Lee Hutchinson, ExecuMed's clinical team specialises in workplace wellbeing, preventative care, and executive medicine.
