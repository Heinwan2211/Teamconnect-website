The Experience

The Streets Become Your Playing Field

The streets become your playing field. In this high-intensity urban pursuit experience, teams are split into hunters and fugitives — each armed with nothing but their wits, a set of encrypted clues, and a rapidly shrinking time window. Communication breaks down. Leadership emerges. Trust gets tested in real time. The Fugitive is built on the principle that pressure reveals character — and that the teams who adapt fastest, win. Fast-moving, unpredictable, and deeply competitive, it runs across Cape Town's urban and outdoor environments and scales from 20 to 500 participants. No two runs play out the same way.
