Escape Labs — Rouge

Table-Top Team Building Reinvented

Experience Overview

More Fun Than You Can Imagine.

Team Connect, in collaboration with HintHunt, brings you Rouge — an exhilarating table-top escape room-style experience designed to energise and unite your team. At their tables, teams race against the clock to crack codes, solve puzzles, and uncover the secrets of Rouge before time runs out. It's competitive, creative, and guaranteed to bring out the best in everyone. Fully hosted by skilled Team Connect and HintHunt facilitators, Rouge travels to your venue — whether it's your office, a conference centre, or an event space anywhere in South Africa. No two teams crack the code the same way.
