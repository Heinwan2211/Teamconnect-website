Culture Throwdown

Culture & Identity — Loud, Funny & Surprisingly Reflective

Experience Overview

Your Culture. Your Identity. Your Throwdown.

A high-energy, game-show-style experience that unpacks workplace culture through competitive play. Teams go head-to-head across fast-paced rounds — culture trivia, values-based dilemmas, read-the-room scenarios, and live debates on workplace norms — all driven by a slick live scoreboard and MC energy. The twist: each round is designed to surface unspoken cultural assumptions, spark genuine conversation, and celebrate what makes a team's identity unique. Loud, funny, and surprisingly reflective. Runs beautifully at any Western Cape conferencing venue or lodge setting — and travels anywhere in South Africa.
