Innovation & Rapid Prototyping — Shark Tank Meets Maker Lab

Experience Overview

Real Briefs. Real Ideas. Real Results.

A design challenge where mixed teams are handed a real-world brief — local, relevant, and slightly uncomfortable — and must ideate, build, and pitch a solution in a compressed time window. Think Shark Tank meets maker lab: unconventional materials, AI-assisted brainstorming tools, and a panel of surprise investors to pitch to at the end. Rooted in the 2026 push toward immersive, hands-on learning over passive presentations. The Western Cape setting adds genuine edge — briefs can be drawn from local challenges like water security, tourism innovation, or township enterprise development. We travel to any venue across South Africa.
