### WHAT TO EXPECT ON THE DAY

You and your team will arrive to a venue already set up for action — tables laid out with mysterious sealed boxes, clue cards face-down, and a leaderboard ready to go live. After a warm welcome and briefing from your Team Connect facilitator, you'll be divided into teams of five and seated at your tables. The energy in the room will already be electric.

When the timer starts, the real fun begins. Your team will work together to crack codes, solve puzzles, interpret cryptic clues, and unravel a narrative mystery — all from your seats in this thrilling table-top escape room experience. Every table is its own team, racing simultaneously against all the others. Quick thinking, clear communication, and creative problem-solving will be your greatest assets.

After 90 minutes of intense, focused challenge, you'll break for a brief refresh before reconvening for the awards and debrief. The leaderboard will reveal who cracked the most clues, and your facilitator will guide a reflection conversation that connects the experience to how your team actually works together. It's fun, it's revealing, and it's genuinely useful.

By the time you leave, your team will have shared something memorable — the kind of experience that becomes an inside reference point for months to come.

### FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

**Q: Do we need any special knowledge or skills to do this?**
Not at all. Escape Labs is designed for all personality types and skill sets. The puzzles are varied — some require logical thinking, some lateral thinking, some creativity. Every person on your team will find their moment to shine.

**Q: What happens if our team gets completely stuck?**
You'll have hint request cards available throughout the experience. Using a hint is completely fine — it keeps the energy up and the experience moving. Your Team Connect facilitator is also on hand throughout.

**Q: Is this competitive? What if some teams finish much faster than others?**
Yes, it's competitive — and that's part of the fun. Teams that finish their puzzle first continue working on bonus challenges to maximise their score. Nobody sits idle, and the leaderboard keeps everyone engaged until the final reveal.

**Q: Can people with mobility considerations participate fully?**
Yes. This is a fully table-top experience — everything is done from a seated position. It is one of our most inclusive formats and works well for teams with mixed physical abilities.

**Q: How do you ensure fair team composition?**
Team Connect will work with you beforehand to structure team composition in a way that creates balanced, mixed groups — cross-departmental or cross-level, depending on your preference. We'll advise on the best approach for your specific group.

### GETTING THE MOST FROM YOUR EXPERIENCE

**Brief your team in advance.** Let them know the day is about collaboration and healthy competition — not individual performance. The teams that win are almost always the ones that communicate best, not the smartest individuals.

**Mix up your team composition.** If you have the opportunity to choose, group people from different departments or levels. Mixed teams surface the most interesting dynamics and generate the most genuine connection.

**Stay present on the day.** Encourage your team to put phones away during the experience. The 90-minute puzzle window goes fast, and full presence makes a real difference to the outcome — and the enjoyment.

**Use the debrief.** The post-experience conversation is where the real insights land. Encourage your team to be honest about what they noticed about how they worked together. It's where the fun becomes genuinely useful.
