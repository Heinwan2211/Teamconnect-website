The Experience

Race. Think. Win. Together.

Inspired by the global phenomenon, Team Connect's Amazing Race puts your team through a multi-stage, multi-challenge adventure across a spectacular Cape Town or Western Cape setting. Teams race between waypoints — cracking codes, completing physical challenges, navigating unfamiliar terrain, and making split-second decisions that affect everyone. The format is deliberately designed to equalise — the fastest runners don't always win. The teams that communicate, delegate, and think together do. High energy, high stakes, and genuinely unforgettable. Scales from 20 to 1000 participants across indoor, outdoor, and hybrid formats.
