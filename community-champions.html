CSR & Social Impact — Leave Something Tangible Behind

The Experience

Team Building That Matters Beyond the Day.

Purpose-led team building that leaves something tangible behind — the highest-trending category in corporate events for 2026. Teams compete to design and execute a micro community project within a set time: assembling food garden kits for a local school, building habitat structures for a fynbos restoration site, or packing care packages for a partner NGO. The competitive layer runs through an impact scoring system — creativity, execution, community relevance, and team storytelling. Closes with a handover ceremony and a short documentary-style recap reel. A natural fit for the Western Cape's rich conservation and community landscape — and deployable across South Africa.
