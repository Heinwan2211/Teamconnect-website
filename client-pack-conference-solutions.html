A Message from Team Connect

A great conference has great speakers — but the moments between the presentations, and the energy in the room when speakers are done, are what determine whether delegates leave feeling genuinely transformed or simply well-informed. Team Connect's Conference Solutions are designed to do one thing: keep your audience fully present, actively engaged, and meaningfully connected across the arc of your event. We are proud to be trusted with those moments, and we'll make every one count.

What to Expect on the Day

Conference Solutions are a suite of six activation formats, each designed to serve a different moment in your conference programme. You may choose a single format for a specific slot, or a combination of formats deployed across a full conference day or multi-day event.

Morning Ignition (15–30 minutes | Conference opening or post-registration) wakes up the room, creates immediate connection between delegates, and sets the tone for a day that is active and participatory — before the first speaker takes the stage.

Post-Lunch Reset (20–30 minutes | After lunch or any energy dip) combats the post-lunch energy dip with movement, laughter, and a burst of connection that gets delegates alert, present, and ready for the afternoon programme.

Table-to-Table Networking (30–45 minutes | Mid-morning or mid-afternoon) is a structured networking experience that gives delegates a reason and a format to connect beyond their immediate table — breaking silos and creating cross-delegate relationships that outlast the conference.

Values Integration (45–60 minutes | Mid-conference or after a keynote on culture or strategy) uses a structured experience to move your conference's core theme or values from concept to lived reality — embedding the day's key message through active participation rather than passive listening.

Creative Stimulus (30–45 minutes | Any point where thinking needs a reset) breaks conventional thinking patterns and opens the room to new ideas through a facilitated creative challenge — particularly effective immediately before a strategy or innovation session.

Closing Synthesis (45–90 minutes | End of conference or final session) is a facilitated closing experience that invites delegates to consolidate, commit, and connect — turning a day of learning and inspiration into a clear set of individual and collective next steps.

Your Team Connect facilitator will read the room continuously and deliver each format with precision timing and professional energy, ensuring every activation feels seamlessly integrated rather than bolted on.

Practical Information

What to Wear

Smart business or smart casual, consistent with the dress code of your conference. No special clothing is required for any of our Conference Solution formats.

What to Bring

No specific items required. Delegates simply need to be present and willing to participate. Stationery and any materials needed for specific formats will be provided by Team Connect.

What We Provide

  • Facilitation team for all selected formats
  • All materials specific to each activation format
  • PA system and AV coordination (or integration with your existing conference AV)
  • Coordination with your conference programme to ensure seamless integration
  • Pre-conference briefing on timing, flow, and facilitator positioning

Dietary & Accessibility

For seated conference formats, full accessibility is standard. For any activation formats involving movement, please inform us of participants with mobility or other accessibility needs at least one week before the event by emailing Info@teamconnect.co.za.

Parking & Arrival

Please direct your Team Connect facilitation team to the venue at least 90 minutes before the conference begins, allowing adequate time for setup and coordination with your AV and events team.

Frequently Asked Questions

Can we pick and choose which formats to use?
Yes. You can select any combination of the six Conference Solution formats. Your Team Connect contact will help you identify which formats suit your programme structure, delegate profile, and conference objectives.
How do you ensure the activations feel integrated rather than separate?
We work closely with your conference organiser in advance to understand the programme flow, the keynote themes, and the desired conference arc. Every activation is briefed and prepared with that context in mind, so it feels like a deliberate part of the programme rather than an interruption.
Can the activations reference our conference theme or specific content?
Absolutely. The more context you share about your conference theme, speakers, and key messages, the more our facilitators can align the activations to reinforce and amplify what's happening in the room. Share your speaker briefs, programme, and theme documents with your Team Connect contact as early as possible.
What if our conference programme is running late?
Our facilitators are trained to adapt timing in real time. Each format has a core experience that can be compressed or extended by 5–10 minutes without compromising quality. We'll always brief your conference organiser on flexible timing options in advance.
Do you provide your own AV, or do you work with our existing setup?
Both options are available. We can provide our own AV infrastructure for standalone activations, or integrate with your existing conference AV setup. We'll discuss the best approach with your conference organiser during the pre-event briefing.

Getting the Most from Your Experience

Share your programme early. The sooner we see your full conference programme, the better we can position each activation for maximum impact. Even a draft schedule helps us plan effectively.

Brief your conference organiser on our role. Ensure your events team knows that Team Connect facilitators will need venue access, AV coordination, and programme timing cooperation. We'll provide a technical rider for this purpose.

Trust the energy reads. Our facilitators are skilled at reading conference energy in real time. Give them the authority to adjust the pace, intensity, or timing of an activation if the room needs it — that responsiveness is what makes Conference Solutions genuinely effective.

Close with intention. If you're using the Closing Synthesis format, invest in the final 10 minutes. That last beat — where delegates articulate their individual commitments and connect them to the collective — is what turns a good conference into a transformative one. Protect that time.
